Think of it as Chicken Soup for the Soulless.

The Inspirational Atheist is a book of quotations on the meaning of life, the mysteries of the universe, love, freedom and the big questions, all asked and answered without resorting to superstition, religion, or any pleas to abandon reason — just good old common sense, logic, and beauty.
